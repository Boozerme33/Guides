### September 24, 2024
##extractors

@flatfile/plugin-zip-extractor@0.6.0
This release updates several Flatfile dependencies, including @flatfile/plugin-job-handler, @flatfile/util-file-buffer, and @flatfile/util-common, to their latest versions. The @flatfile/plugin-zip-extractor package itself has been updated from version 0.5.4 to 0.6.0. The peerDependencies have also been updated, with @flatfile/listener being updated to ^1.0.5. Additionally, the development dependency @flatfile/utils-testing has been updated to ^0.3.0.
