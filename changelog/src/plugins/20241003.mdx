### October 03, 2024
##transform

@flatfile/plugin-dedupe@1.2.1
This release updates the @flatfile/plugin-dedupe package to version 1.2.1. The @flatfile/api dependency is updated to improve regional support. The @flatfile/plugin-job-handler and @flatfile/util-common dependencies are also updated to their latest versions 0.6.1 and 1.4.1 respectively. The peerDependencies @flatfile/api and @flatfile/listener are updated to versions 1.9.19 and 1.1.0 respectively.
